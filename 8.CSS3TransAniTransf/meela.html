Start

(Center Image)

This is Meela Zendejas. Her start to life has been a continuous uphill battle. Meela was born at 27 due to complications with Mom. However, due to additional conplications, Meela suffered from IUGR (Intrauterine growth restriction) and only measured at 20 weeks and weighing only 15 Oz.

(Left Image)

After multiple failed attempts to remove her breathing tube, doctors decided that a tracheostomy was needed. She still has her trach to this day.

(Right Image)

Although she has been through so much in her early days, her personality shined. As the days went on in the NICU, we started to see her sassy and loving personality bloom to what it is today.
